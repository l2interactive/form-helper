Class: FormHelperRequest

formHelper.FormHelperRequest

new FormHelperRequest(formEl, rule, submitEvent)

FormHelperRequest instances are created automatically by FormHelper when form matching any registered FormRule submits. FormHelperRequest is not typically instantiated directly by the user.

The FormRule's callbacks are executed in the context of the FormHelperRequest instance, so members listed below are available during any XHR callbacks and FormRule.StatusHandler
Parameters:

formEl HTMLFormElement Form element to be handled
